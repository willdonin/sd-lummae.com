The Heart Behind the Glow

Lummae was born from a love for nature, creativity, and meaningful connections. Founded by Danna Perez, Lummae specializes in hand-poured soy wax candles housed in beautifully crafted concrete vessels. Each candle is made with care and intention, using only natural, eco-friendly materials to create a product that is as gentle on the earth as it is captivating. Lummae’s candles go beyond fragrance—they carry messages of inspiration, hope, and love.

Danna’s journey, from running a successful organic business in Colombia to creating Lummae in the U.S., reflects her dedication to quality and her passion for creating products that resonate with people on a deeper level. Whether you’re looking to elevate your space or gift something truly special, Lummae invites you to experience the warmth and beauty of handmade candles that light up the soul.
